Research Interests

I am mainly interested in control theory and micro-swimming.
I am currently studying the controllability of parabolic systems of PDEs.
I also work on controllability in finite dimension, applied to micro-swimmers, which are microscopic objects that move and propel in a fluid. I have studied a simple model of a magnetized micro-swimmer, in order to understand its dynamics and establish local controllability conditions on more general control systems.
