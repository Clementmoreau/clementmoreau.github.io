Bio

I am a former student at Ecole Normale Supérieure de Cachan (France).

Research Interests

I am interested in control theory and numerical analysis. My research focuses on micro-swimmers, which are microscopic objects that move and propel in a fluid. The micro-swimming research field lies at the crossing between mathematics, cellular biology and micro-engineering. I am currently studying a simple model of a magnetised micro-swimmer, in order to understand its dynamics and establish local controllability conditions.
